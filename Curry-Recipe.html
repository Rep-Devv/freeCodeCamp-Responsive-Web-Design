Curry

This is my first interactive recipe page. I chose curry because it's a complex dish that allows me to organize a lot of information using ordered lists for the steps and unordered lists for the ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 500g of chicken breast
  • 2 tablespoons of curry paste (red, green or yellow)
  • 1 can of coconut milk (400ml)
  • 1 diced onion
  • 2 sliced carrots
  • 1 red pepper
  • Oil, salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Sauté: In a large frying pan with a little oil, brown the onion and chicken until well cooked.
  2. Spices: Add the curry paste and stir for a minute to release all its aroma.
  3. Vegetables: Add the carrots and bell pepper. Cook for about 5 minutes over medium heat.
  4. Liquids: Pour in the coconut milk and mix well. Let it simmer gently.
  5. Reduction: Lower the heat and let it cook for 10 minutes until the sauce thickens slightly.
  6. Serve: Serve with white rice and enjoy your creation.
